## The problem this solves

The incident record is the last thing to get attention during a live incident and the first thing asked for in a post-incident review, a regulatory audit or a service board meeting. The gap between what a skilled service manager carries in their head and what gets written down is enormous — and it costs time, credibility and sometimes regulatory standing.

This tool bridges that gap.
